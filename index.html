ABOUT US

Hayath Medicare Group of Companies

Hayath Medicare Group of Companies is a diversified healthcare organization committed to delivering accessible, affordable, and quality healthcare solutions. Built on more than five years of experience in hospital and healthcare management, the group operates across hospitals, community clinics, pharmaceutical manufacturing and distribution, and healthcare education. Headquartered in Kuttippuram, Malappuram, Hayath Medicare focuses strongly on serving rural and underserved communities. Through an integrated ecosystem and ethical practices, the group ensures consistent standards of care across all its ventures. Driven by visionary leadership and skilled professionals, Hayath Medicare continues to create employment opportunities while strengthening regional healthcare infrastructure. Our goal is to build healthier communities and a sustainable future through trusted healthcare services.

Hayath Medicare LLP – Super Specialty Hospitals and Diagnostic Centres

Hayath Medicare LLP is the flagship hospital division of the Hayath Medicare Group, delivering advanced and compassionate healthcare through its super specialty hospitals and diagnostic centres. Established with a mission to strengthen rural healthcare, the hospital has grown into a multi-disciplinary medical institution equipped with modern diagnostics and advanced treatment facilities. With a strong team of experienced doctors, nurses, and healthcare professionals, Hayath Medicare LLP offers comprehensive patient-centered care across multiple specialties. The hospital integrates technology with clinical excellence to ensure accurate diagnosis and effective treatment. Focused on affordability and accessibility, it brings quality healthcare closer to the community. Hayath Medicare LLP continues to expand its capacity and services to meet the growing healthcare needs of the region.

Hayath Medicare Clinics LLP

Hayath Medicare Clinics LLP is a community-focused healthcare initiative under the Hayath Medicare Group of Companies, established to make quality medical care accessible at the grassroots level. Through a growing network of clinics located within and around Kuttippuram, the clinics deliver essential primary and specialty healthcare services closer to patients’ homes. Each clinic is supported by trained medical professionals and basic diagnostic facilities to ensure timely and effective care. With a strong emphasis on preventive healthcare and early diagnosis, the clinics help reduce the burden on hospitals. Hayath Medicare Clinics LLP is committed to affordability, trust, and continuity of care. The initiative plays a vital role in strengthening rural healthcare infrastructure and improving community wellbeing.

Hayath Medicare Institute LLP

Hayath Medicare Institute LLP is the educational arm of the Hayath Medicare Group of Companies, dedicated to developing skilled and compassionate healthcare professionals. The institute offers paramedical and non-paramedical courses designed to meet the evolving needs of the healthcare industry. With a strong focus on practical training and hands-on clinical exposure, students gain real-world experience within Hayath Medicare hospitals and clinics. Experienced faculty and industry-aligned curriculum ensure quality education and professional readiness. The institute emphasizes affordability and accessibility for students across Kerala. Through education and skill development, Hayath Medicare Institute LLP contributes to employment generation and the long-term strengthening of the healthcare sector.

Hayath Medicare Pharma Care LLP

Hayath Medicare Pharma Care LLP is the pharmaceutical and surgical distribution and manufacturing division of the Hayath Medicare Group of Companies. The venture is committed to supplying safe, effective, and high-quality healthcare products that meet strict regulatory and quality standards. Operating from Kuttippuram, it supports hospitals, clinics, and pharmacies with reliable medicines and medical consumables. Strong quality assurance systems and ethical sourcing practices ensure product consistency and safety. Efficient logistics and supply chain management enable timely availability across the region. Through affordability and trust, Hayath Medicare Pharma Care LLP strengthens the healthcare ecosystem and supports better patient outcomes.

Hayath Mother and Child Care LLP

Hayath Mother and Child Care LLP is a specialized healthcare facility dedicated to comprehensive gynecological, maternity, and pediatric care in Kuttippuram. Established in 2024, the center offers advanced medical services supported by modern diagnostics, emergency care, and specialized treatment units. Facilities include a state-of-the-art scanning center, IVF services, pediatric and gynecological emergency care, and wellness support through yoga and nutrition programs. Designed with patient comfort in mind, the center provides private suites, child-friendly spaces, and supportive family amenities. Combining world-class standards with affordability, Hayath Mother and Child Care LLP delivers a unique and trusted healthcare experience for women and children in the Malabar region.
