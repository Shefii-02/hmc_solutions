Company Overview

About Hayath Medicare

Established in 2025, Hayath Medicare Group of Companies stands as a trusted name in the healthcare industry, built on more than five years of excellence and experience in hospital and healthcare management. Rooted in Kuttippuram, Malappuram, the group was founded with a clear vision ‚Äî to make quality healthcare accessible, affordable, and advanced, especially for rural communities.

Our journey began with Hayath Medicare LLP, a full-fledged super specialty hospital delivering advanced medical care. Building on this foundation, we expanded into underserved regions through Hayath Medicare Clinics LLP, operating a growing network of clinics within a 45 km radius of Kuttippuram, ensuring essential primary and specialty healthcare closer to home.

To strengthen the healthcare ecosystem further, we are establishing Hayath Medicare Pharma Care LLP, focusing on pharmaceutical and surgical distribution and manufacturing. Complementing this, Hayath Medicare Institute LLP is being developed to provide paramedical and non-paramedical education, nurturing skilled professionals and supporting employment generation across Kerala.

Our flagship entity, Hayath Medicare Solutions Private Limited, serves as the central pillar of operations. Headquartered in Kuttippuram, it is guided by experienced board members, skilled executives, and visionary leadership committed to sustainable growth, local economic development, and community wellbeing.

Looking ahead, Hayath Medicare Group aims to emerge as a leading healthcare provider across Malappuram and beyond, while exploring opportunities in health tourism. At Hayath Medicare, we build lasting trust with patients, partners, and investors ‚Äî shaping a healthier, stronger, and more prosperous future for all.
